Super Ale Bros. Redux

Description

Welcome to Super Ale Bros. Redux, a heavily modified ASM hack of Super Mario Bros.

This hack features:
  • Backwards scrolling.
  • P-Switches.
  • On/Off blocks.
  • Textboxes.
  • 32 main levels + 4 kaizo levels, totaling 9 worlds.
  • Shell grabbing.
  • Themed worlds.
  • Saving.
  • A pause menu.
  • Shell-block interactions.
  • Console compatible.
  • And more!
